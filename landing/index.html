Overview

Athena was established as a way of modifying the bureaucratic nature of doctor consultations. It has received global attention and has so far managed to recruit over 10,000 licensed physicians in its affiliate program. Furthermore, a total of 2 million consultations have been booked online and via our app. As of lately, we have had to reduce our member footprint due to excessive demand, thus we have implemented a waiting list, which you can access via the survey below. We look forward to welcoming you to our platform very soon!
